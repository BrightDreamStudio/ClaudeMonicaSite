The Two Deaths and the One Worth Dying For

The Book

Sometimes, a part of us needs to die so we can move on — renewed, lighter, and truly alive. Letting go of what no longer serves us clears the path for a new beginning. When we're ready to release our emotional baggage, it’s like going through a rebirth.

About the Book

What happens after death? Where does our soul go? These questions lie at the heart of an unforgettable journey. Through deeply personal experiences, Monica Hards explores near-death, sensitivity, and spiritual awakening. This book invites you to go beyond what the mind can grasp — to believe in the unseen, trust your intuition, and break free from patterns of pain and fear. With compassion and clarity, she opens a door to transformation, connection, and living with presence.

Dying to Live is an invitation to discover your soul’s deepest truth.
